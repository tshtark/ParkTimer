# Privacy Policy

**ParkTimer — Parking Meter Alert**

Last updated: April 9, 2026

## Summary

ParkTimer does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Everything stays on your device.

## Data Collection

ParkTimer collects **no data whatsoever**. Specifically:

- **No analytics** — We do not track how you use the app.
- **No advertising** — There are no ads, no ad networks, no tracking pixels.
- **No accounts** — There is no sign-up, login, or user profile.
- **No network requests** — The app makes zero connections to any server. It works entirely offline.
- **No third-party SDKs** — No Firebase, no Amplitude, no Mixpanel, no anything.

## Data Stored on Your Device

ParkTimer stores the following data **locally on your iPhone only**:

- **Parking sessions** — Start time, duration, and location of your parking sessions, saved as JSON files in the app's private documents directory.
- **Photos** — If you take a photo of your parking spot, it is saved locally in the app's documents directory. It is never uploaded anywhere.
- **Settings** — Your preferences (alert timing, sounds, vehicle type) are saved in UserDefaults on your device.
- **Purchase status** — Whether you have purchased ParkTimer Pro is stored locally via Apple's StoreKit framework.

This data is not accessible to us or any third party. It is not backed up to iCloud (unless you have enabled device-level iCloud backups, which is controlled by your iOS settings).

## Location Data

ParkTimer uses your location **only when the app is open** ("When In Use" permission) to:

1. Save your car's GPS coordinates when you start a parking session
2. Calculate walking distance and time back to your car
3. Reverse geocode your coordinates to a street address (using Apple's on-device geocoding)

Your location is **never transmitted** to any server. It is stored only in your local parking session data.

## Notifications

ParkTimer sends local notifications to alert you before your parking meter expires. These notifications are generated entirely on your device. No push notification servers are involved.

## In-App Purchases

ParkTimer Pro is a one-time in-app purchase processed entirely through Apple's App Store. We do not have access to your payment information.

## Children's Privacy

ParkTimer does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13.

## Changes

If we ever change this policy, we will update this page with a new "Last updated" date.
