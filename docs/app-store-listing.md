## Description

ParkTimer counts down your parking meter right on your Lock Screen and Dynamic Island. Get alerted before time runs out — even when your phone is in your pocket.

SIMPLE AND FAST
Pick your meter time (15m, 30m, 1h, 2h, or custom), tap Start, and you're done. Your car's location is saved automatically. Quick Restart lets daily parkers start with one tap.

ALWAYS VISIBLE
Your remaining time stays on your Lock Screen and Dynamic Island as a Live Activity. No need to open the app to check — just glance at your phone.

FIND YOUR CAR
Forgot where you parked? ParkTimer saves your GPS location, shows it on a map, and gives you walking directions. Take a photo of your spot. Share your car's location with someone who needs to find it.

SMART ALERTS
Get notified before your meter expires. ParkTimer Pro calculates your walking distance and alerts you with enough time to get back — so you never arrive to a ticket on your windshield.

TRACK YOUR PARKING
See your full parking history. Track how much you spend with the built-in cost tracker. View monthly statistics: sessions, hours parked, and total cost.

PAY ONCE, OWN FOREVER
ParkTimer is free with full functionality. Upgrade to Pro for a one-time $4.99 purchase — no subscriptions, no ads, no accounts, no data collected. Ever.
